About Motivision Media

Real Estate Media, Done Right.

We're a Hudson Valley real estate media company built around one idea: your listing's first showing happens online, so it has to look incredible there. We make the photography, drone, and video that get agents more saves, more showings, and faster sales.

The Person Behind It

Built By Someone Who Gets It.

I'm Kerby Emile, founder of Motivision Media and a licensed New York real estate agent. I started this company because I kept seeing the same problem: agents with incredible listings who were invisible online.

Because I work in real estate, I'm not just a photographer who shows up with a camera. I know what buyers look for when they're scrolling Zillow, what makes them stop and book a showing, and what agents actually need on listing day to hit the market fast.

Every shoot is produced with that lens — MLS-ready photos, drone aerials, cinematic video, and social content that keeps working long after the deal closes. That's the difference between media that documents a home and media that sells it.
